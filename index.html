About ParkSense AI

Building the Future of Smart Parking

ParkSense AI is an innovative parking platform that uses Artificial Intelligence, live parking data, and intelligent navigation to help drivers quickly locate available parking spaces. Our mission is to reduce traffic congestion, save time, reduce fuel consumption, and make city travel more efficient.

Our Story

Hi! I am Arjun Dalvi, a Class 7 student of MIS International School, Pune. I founded ParkSense AI because finding parking in busy cities often wastes valuable time and fuel. I wanted to create an intelligent solution that helps drivers quickly find available parking spaces using Artificial Intelligence. This project represents my passion for technology, innovation, and solving real-world problems. My dream is to make ParkSense AI a trusted smart parking platform that benefits drivers and supports the development of smarter cities.
